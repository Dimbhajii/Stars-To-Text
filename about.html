About Us

We believe communication is a right, not a privilege.

Sign2Text was created to bridge the communication gap between deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals and the hearing world. We believe that technology should empower everyone equally, and that includes making everyday interactions more natural and accessible for the deaf community.

Our team combines expertise in artificial intelligence, accessible design, and real-world user experience to build tools that genuinely make a difference. Sign2Text uses browser-based hand tracking and gesture recognition to translate sign language into expressive on-screen text — no specialist hardware required.

What We Stand For

Accessibility is not an afterthought — it is built into everything we create. We design for the people who need it most, and we measure success by how naturally and confidently they can communicate using our product.
