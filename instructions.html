Hand Gesture Guide

Learn the hand gestures recognised by Sign2Text. Show your hand clearly to the camera and hold each gesture steady.

How to Use

1

Allow camera access and position your hand in front of the webcam.

2

Form a hand gesture from the list below and hold it steady.

3

The system will recognise your gesture and display the corresponding message.

4

Make sure your hand is well-lit and clearly visible to the camera.

5

Hold gestures steady for best recognition accuracy.

Supported Gestures

🤌

What Do You Want?

All fingertips bunched together near thumb tip. Create a pinching gesture with all fingers touching.

🤟

I Love You

Thumb, index finger, and pinky extended upward. Middle and ring fingers curled down.

👌

Okay!

Peace sign. Index and middle fingers extended upward in a V. Ring and pinky fingers curled down.

☝️

Hello

Index finger pointing straight up. All other fingers curled down into a fist.

🤙

Thank You

Only pinky finger extended. Index, middle, and ring fingers curled into palm.

🖐️

This Is Nadim

Open hand with all fingers extended and spread apart. Hold hand at chest area.

🤝

Nice to Meet You

Both hands with only index fingers extended, tips touching or very close together.

🖕

Fuck U

Middle finger extended upward. All other fingers curled down.

✊

Fist (Repel)

Closed fist. All fingers curled tightly into palm. Moves nearby particles away from your hand.

Tips for Best Results

  • Ensure your hand is well-lit and clearly visible to the camera
  • Hold gestures steady for at least half a second for recognition
  • Keep your hand at a comfortable distance from the camera (about arm's length)
  • Avoid cluttered backgrounds that may interfere with hand detection
  • Make gestures clearly and deliberately for best accuracy
