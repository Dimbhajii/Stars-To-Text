Our Research

At Sign2Text, our research focuses on improving communication accessibility for deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals. Many people who use British Sign Language (BSL) face challenges when interacting with others who do not understand sign language, especially in fast-paced environments such as cafes, restaurants, and customer service settings. This communication gap can lead to misunderstandings, delays, and reduced independence.

Studies and real-world observations show that not all deaf individuals rely on written communication, and many prefer to use sign language as their primary method of expression.

However, a lack of awareness and training among the general public creates barriers. This highlights the need for a simple, effective solution that supports real-time communication without requiring others to learn sign language.

Our Solution

Sign2Text uses Artificial Intelligence and hand gesture recognition to translate sign language into text instantly. This allows deaf users to communicate naturally while the system converts their gestures into readable text on screen. Our research shows that real-time translation can significantly improve confidence, independence, and overall user experience.

For businesses, particularly in the hospitality sector, communication is essential for providing good customer service. Miscommunication can lead to incorrect orders, frustration, and loss of customers. By implementing Sign2Text, businesses can create a more inclusive environment, improve service efficiency, and demonstrate commitment to accessibility and equality.

Key Benefits of Sign2Text

  • For Businesses
    • Improved customer service and satisfaction
    • More inclusive environment for all customers
    • Enhanced service efficiency and speed
    • Positive reputation and brand image
  • For Users
    • Increased confidence in daily communication
    • Greater independence without needing interpreters
    • Natural sign language communication preserved
    • Better overall service experiences

In addition, accessibility is increasingly recognised as a legal and ethical responsibility. Inclusive businesses are more likely to attract a wider customer base and build a positive reputation. Sign2Text supports this by offering a practical, easy-to-use solution that benefits both customers and staff.

Our Mission

Overall, our research highlights the importance of combining technology with accessibility to remove communication barriers. Sign2Text aims to create a more inclusive society where deaf individuals can communicate confidently and businesses can provide equal service to all customers.
